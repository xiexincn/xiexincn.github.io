👤 About Me 个人简介

I am an Associate Professor, with College of Intelligence and Computing (opens new window), Tianjin University. Prior to joining Tianjin University, I was a researcher in the Hong Kong Theory Lab of Huawei 2012 laboratory from 2022 to 2023. From 2019 to 2022, I also worked as a postdoctoral fellow in the PEIlab (opens new window) of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I received my Ph.D. degree (2019) and B. Eng. degree (2013) in computer science from Dalian University of Technology.

My research area includes internet of things(IoT) and Edge AI, with a particular focus on wireless sensing, RFID, and federated learning.

Five Representive Publications 代表论文

Frequency-and Orientation-related Phase Fingerprints for RFID Tag Authentication (opens new window)

K Zhang, J Zhang, X Xie*, X Tong, X Liu, K Li

In Proceedings of IEEE SECON 2022, CCF-B, best paper candidate

Contribution: We are the first to uses the phase values of tags at different frequencies and orientations to construct physical-layer fingerprints to eliminate the influence of environmental factors.

Federated unlearning via class-discriminative pruning (opens new window)

J Wang, S Guo, X Xie*, H Qi

In Proceedings of ACM Web Conference 2022, CCF-A

Contribution: We are the first to uses the channel pruning to achive class-level unlearning solution in federated learning scenario.

arXiv (opens new window)

Protect Privacy from Gradient Leakage Attack in Federated Learning (opens new window)

J Wang, S Guo, X Xie*, H Qi

In Proceedings of IEEE INFOCOM 2022, CCF-A

Contribution: We present a novel defense method, whose intuition is perturbing gradients to match information leakage risk such that the defense overhead is lightweight while privacy protection is adequate.

In Proceedings of IEEE INFOCOM 2021, CCF-A
