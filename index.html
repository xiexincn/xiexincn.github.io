I am currently a Professor at the College of Intelligence and Computing (opens new window), Tianjin University, a position I have held since 2025. Prior to this, I served as an Associate Professor at Tianjin University from 2023 to 2024. Before joining academia, I was a Researcher at Huawei 2012 Laboratories' Hong Kong Theory Lab (2022-2023) and a Postdoctoral Fellow at the PEIlab (opens new window) of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (2019-2022). I earned both my Ph.D. (2019) and B.Eng. (2013) degrees in Computer Science from Dalian University of Technology.

My research area includes Internet of Things(IoT) and Edge AI, with a particular focus on Mobile Sensing, and Federated learning.
